# Copywriting Skills (文案技巧)

## Overview

Copywriting is writing engaging, authentic, and valuable content that resonates with your Xiaohongshu audience, using conversational tone, emotional connection, and clear structure to drive engagement and action.

## When to Use

**Use when**:
- Writing post body content
- Creating product descriptions
- Developing brand voice
- Writing educational or tutorial content
- Crafting emotional or personal stories

**Do NOT use when**:
- Writing headlines (use title-writing)
- Creating scripts (use script-writing)

## Core Pattern

**Before** (boring copy):
```
❌ "这个产品很好用,推荐大家购买"
❌ Formal, distant tone
❌ Generic praise
❌ No personality
❌ No emotional connection
```

**After** (engaging copy):
```
✅ "OMG姐妹们!我终于找到了学生党福音..."
✅ Conversational, excited tone
✅ Personal experience shared
✅ Authentic personality
✅ Emotional resonance
```

**5 Copywriting Elements**:
1. **Hook Opening** - Grab attention immediately
2. **Story/Context** - Set up relevance
3. **Value Delivery** - Deliver promised content
4. **Emotional Connection** - Relate to audience
5. **Call to Action** - Tell what to do next

## Quick Reference

| Content Type | Tone | Structure | Length |
|--------------|------|-----------|--------|
| **Tutorial** | Helpful, clear | Problem → Steps → Result | Medium |
| **Review** | Honest, detailed | Hook → Experience → Verdict | Long |
| **Personal** | Vulnerable, authentic | Story → Emotion → Reflection | Medium |
| **Tips** | Energetic, quick | Hook → 3 tips → CTA | Short |

## Implementation

### Step 1: Write Compelling Opening Hook

**Hook types**:
- **Question**: "你还在...吗?"
- **Surprising**: "没想到..."
- **Relatable**: "姐妹们,我也是..."
- **Promise**: "让我告诉你..."

**Examples**:
- ✅ "姐妹们!我终于找到学生党本命防晒了!"
- ✅ "说实话,我用过20多款眼霜,这款彻底颠覆了我的认知"
- ✅ "别再花冤枉钱了,今天来扒一扒平价好物"

### Step 2: Use Conversational Tone

**Xiaohongshu voice**:
- Use "姐妹们", "大家", "宝宝们" (inclusive terms)
- Add emoji: 💖, ✨, 🔥, 😭 (emotionally)
- Be enthusiastic: "OMG", "绝绝子", "真香"
- Keep sentences short and punchy

**Avoid**:
- Formal language
- Academic tone
- Corporate speak
- Distant or cold tone

### Step 3: Structure Content Clearly

**Clear structure**:
```
📌 Opening hook (1-2 sentences)
↓
💡 Main content (3-5 bullet points)
↓
✨ Personal experience or tips
↓
🎯 Conclusion + CTA
```

**Use emojis as bullets** (improves scannability)

### Step 4: Add Authentic Details

**Specificity = authenticity**:
- ✅ "用了2周,每天早晚各一次"
- ✅ "89元,用了3个月"
- ✅ "干皮敏感肌,用了没过敏"
- ❌ "很好用,推荐" (too vague)

### Step 5: Create Emotional Connection

**Share feelings**:
- "真的感动哭了"
- "后悔没早点发现"
- "无限回购"
- "相见恨晚"

**Relate to audience**:
- "学生党党看过来"
- "姐妹们是不是也..."
- "我懂这种感觉"

### Step 6: End With Clear CTA

**Call to action examples**:
- "关注我,不迷路"
- "点赞收藏,下次容易找"
- "评论区告诉我你的看法"
- "主页还有更多好物分享"

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| **Too formal** | Use conversational, friendly tone |
| **Wall of text** | Break into paragraphs, use bullets |
| **No personality** | Share your voice and feelings |
| **Generic praise** | Be specific and authentic |
| **No CTA** | Always tell audience what to do |

## Real-World Impact

**Engaging copy**: 2-3x more comments and shares
**Boring copy**: Skips, low engagement despite good product

**Key metric: Authenticity drives engagement.
