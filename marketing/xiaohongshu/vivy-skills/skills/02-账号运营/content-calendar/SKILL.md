# Content Calendar (内容规划)

## Overview

Content calendar is your strategic posting schedule - planning when to publish what content to maintain consistency, align with audience behavior, and maximize engagement and growth.

## When to Use

**Use when**:
- Scheduling weekly posts
- Planning seasonal content
- Organizing content production
- Maintaining posting consistency
- Coordinating themed series

**Do NOT use when**:
- Planning content topics (use content-planning)
- Choosing posting times (use data-analytics)

## Core Pattern

**Before** (inconsistent posting):
```
❌ Post randomly when remember
❌ 3 posts one day, none for a week
❌ No strategic timing
❌ Miss opportunities
```

**After** (scheduled content):
```
✅ 3-4 posts per week consistently
✅ Posted at optimal times
✅ Aligned with trends and seasons
✅ Content ready in advance
```

**4 Calendar Components**:
1. **Frequency** - How often to post
2. **Timing** - Best times to post
3. **Theming** - Weekly or monthly themes
4. **Flexibility** - Room for timely content

## Quick Reference

| Account Stage | Frequency | Best Time | Content Mix |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **New (<1K)** | 3-4x/week | 7-9pm weekdays | 80% pillars, 20% trends |
| **Growing (1-10K)** | 4-5x/week | Peak hours | 70% pillars, 30% trends |
| **Established (10K+)** | 5-7x/week | Data-optimized | 60% pillars, 40% trends |

## Implementation

### Step 1: Set Posting Frequency

**Recommended by stage**:
- New accounts: 3-4 posts/week (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun)
- Growing: 4-5 posts/week
- Established: 5-7 posts/week

**Quality over quantity**: Better 3 great posts than 5 mediocre ones.

### Step 2: Identify Optimal Times

**General best times**:
- 7-9pm weekdays (after work)
- 9-11am weekends (morning routine)
- Avoid 2-5pm (low activity)

**Your best times**: Use data-analytics to find when YOUR audience is most active.

### Step 3: Plan Weekly Themes

**Example weekly pattern**:
```
Mon: Educational (pillar 1)
Wed: Tutorial or tips (pillar 2)
Fri: Review or recommendation (pillar 3)
Sun: Trending or seasonal topic (flexible)
```

### Step 4: Build Monthly Calendar

**Plan 4 weeks out**:
- Block weekly themes
- Note seasonal opportunities (holidays, events)
- Leave 20% flexible for timely content
- Track what's performed vs. actual

### Step 5: Create Content Pipeline

**Production workflow**:
- Week 1: Create content for Week 3
- Week 2: Create content for Week 4
- Always 1-2 weeks ahead

**Benefits**:
- Never stressed about "what to post"
- Time to refine content quality
- Can batch create similar content

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| **Inconsistent posting** | Use calendar reminders |
| **All content same type** | Rotate through pillars |
| **Planning too rigid** | Leave 20% flexible |
| **Posting at wrong times** | Use your audience data |

## Real-World Impact

**Consistent posters**: 3x faster growth than sporadic posters
**Strategic timing**: 2x higher engagement than random timing
