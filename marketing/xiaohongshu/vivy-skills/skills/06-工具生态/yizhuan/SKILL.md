# Yizhuan (易撰)

## Overview

Yizhuan is an AI-powered content creation platform specifically designed for Chinese social media including Xiaohongshu, offering features such as AI copywriting, content ideation, hot topic discovery, and automated posting to help creators scale content production efficiently while maintaining quality.

## When to Use

**Use when**:
- Generating caption ideas and drafts
- Finding trending content topics
- Automating content scheduling
- Analyzing content performance
- Scaling content production
- Overcoming writer's block
- Creating variations of successful content

**Do NOT use when**:
- Need completely original, human-written content only
- Require deep personal stories (AI lacks authenticity)
- Want to develop unique voice (AI sounds generic)
- Can't review and edit AI output

**Before** (manual content creation):
