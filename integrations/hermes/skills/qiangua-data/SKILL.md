# Qiangua Data (千瓜数据)

## Overview

Qiangua Data is a professional third-party analytics platform for Xiaohongshu, providing deep insights into account performance, competitor analysis, trending content, and audience demographics that go far beyond the platform's native analytics.

## When to Use

**Use when**:
- Need comprehensive account performance analysis
- Researching competitor strategies and performance
- Finding trending topics and hashtags in your niche
- Analyzing audience demographics and behavior
- Identifying top-performing content formats
- Planning content strategy based on data

**Do NOT use when**:
- Just need basic metrics (use Xiaohongshu Creator Center, free)
- Need real-time monitoring during posting
- Analyzing non-Xiaohongshu platforms

## Core Pattern

**Before** (platform analytics only):
```
❌ Limited to native Creator Center data
❌ Can't see competitor performance
❌ No industry benchmarks or trends
❌ Basic metrics only
```

**After** (Qiangua insights):
```
✅ Deep competitor analysis (top posts, growth rates)
✅ Trending topics and hashtags in real-time
✅ Industry benchmarks and standards
✅ Advanced audience segmentation
```

**6 Core Analytics Capabilities**:
1. **Account Analysis** - Comprehensive performance tracking
2. **Competitor Research** - Spy on any account's strategy
3. **Trend Discovery** - Hot topics, rising hashtags
4. **Content Analysis** - Top-performing formats and styles
5. **Audience Insights** - Detailed demographics and behavior
6. **Industry Benchmarks** - Compare against category standards

**Get started**:

4. Link your Xiaohongshu account (for analytics tracking)

### Step 2: Analyze Your Own Account

**Navigate to Account Analysis**:
1. Dashboard → "Account Analysis" (账号分析)
2. Enter your account name or URL
3. View comprehensive dashboard

**Key Metrics to Track**:

**Growth Metrics**:
- **Follower growth rate** (weekly/monthly)
- **Growth trend** (upward, flat, declining)
- **Growth acceleration** (speed of growth)

**Engagement Metrics**:
- **Average engagement rate** (compare to industry benchmark)
- **Top performing posts** (by views, likes, comments, shares)
- **Best posting times** (heatmap of hourly performance)

**Content Performance**:
- **Content type breakdown** (image vs video vs carousel)
- **Top topics** (which categories perform best)
- **Hashtag effectiveness** (which tags drive most reach)

**Action**: Export monthly report (PDF/Excel) for tracking progress.

### Step 3: Research Competitors

**Find and Analyze Competitors**:

**Identify competitors**:
1. Search your niche on Xiaohongshu
2. Find accounts with 10K-100K followers (realistic benchmarks)
3. Select 5-10 competitors for ongoing tracking

**Analyze each competitor**:
1. Qiangua → "Account Analysis"
2. Enter competitor account name
3. Study dashboard:

**What to learn from competitors**:
- **Content strategy**: What topics do they post?
- **Posting frequency**: How often do they post?
- **Top posts**: What made their best content succeed?
- **Growth trajectory**: How fast are they growing?
- **Engagement tactics**: How do they engage with audience?

**Competitor Analysis Template**:
```

Followers: XX,XXX

Top 3 Content Topics:
1. [Topic] - Avg engagement: X%

- [Factor 1]
- [Factor 2]
```

### Step 4: Discover Trending Topics

**Navigate to Trend Discovery**:
1. Dashboard → "Hot Topics" (热门话题) or "Trending" (趋势)
2. Filter by your category (fashion, beauty, food, etc.)
3. Sort by: Rising (上升最快), Hot (最热), New (最新)

**Use trending data for content ideas**:

**Rising hashtags** (up-and-coming):
- Identify hashtags with >100% growth in 7 days
- Early adoption advantage (less competition)
- Example: "#韩系穿搭" up 300% this week

**Hot topics** (currently popular):
- High search volume, high engagement
- More competitive but larger reach
- Example: "#平价好物" consistently hot

**Niche opportunities**:
- Filter by subcategory (e.g., "skincare for sensitive skin")
- Find underserved areas with high demand
- Less competition, dedicated audience

**Action**: Create content around 2-3 trending topics weekly.

### Step 5: Analyze Industry Benchmarks

**Navigate to Industry Data**:
1. Dashboard → "Industry Analysis" (行业分析)
2. Select your category (e.g., "Fashion > Korean Style")
3. View industry-wide metrics

**Key Benchmark Metrics**:

**Engagement Benchmarks**:
- **Industry avg engagement rate**: 8-12%
- **Top 10% avg engagement rate**: 15-20%
- **Your engagement rate**: Compare and identify gaps

**Growth Benchmarks**:
- **Industry avg monthly growth**: 5-10%
- **Fast-growing accounts**: 15-30% monthly
- **Your growth rate**: Are you above or below average?

**Content Performance**:
- **Best posting times**: Industry-wide patterns
- **Top content formats**: Image vs video vs carousel
- **Most effective hashtags**: Industry-standard tags

**Use benchmarks to**:
- Set realistic goals (e.g., "Reach industry avg engagement in 3 months")
- Identify underperforming areas (e.g., "My videos below industry benchmark")
- Learn from top performers (study top 10% in niche)

### Step 6: Deep Audience Analysis

**Navigate to Audience Insights**:
1. Account Analysis → "Audience" (受众分析)
2. View detailed demographics

**Audience Demographics**:

**Age distribution**:
- Breakdown by age groups (18-24, 25-30, 31-40, etc.)
- Compare to your content (are you reaching target?)
- Adjust content style if mismatch

**Gender split**:
- Percentage male vs female
- Align with your niche (e.g., fashion may skew female)
- Tailor content if targeting different balance

**Geographic distribution**:
- Top cities/regions
- Localize content if concentrated in specific areas

**Interests and behavior**:
- **Interest tags**: What else do they like?
- **Active hours**: When are they online?
- **Engagement style**: Do they comment more or save more?

**Use audience data to**:
- Refine content strategy (match audience preferences)
- Optimize posting schedule (post when they're active)
- Choose relevant collaboration partners (brands targeting same audience)

### Step 7: Generate Content Strategy Report

**Monthly Strategy Report** (using Qiangua data):

**Section 1: Performance Summary**
- Follower growth: X% (vs industry avg Y%)

**Section 2: Competitor Insights**

**Section 3: Trending Opportunities**

- Content ideas based on trends

**Section 4: Audience Insights**

- 3 content ideas based on data

- 1 goal for next month

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why Happens | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Only analyzing own account** | Don't see competitor data | Research 5-10 competitors weekly |
| **Ignoring benchmarks** | Focus on absolute numbers | Compare to industry standards |
| **Chasing every trend** | Fear of missing out | Pick trends aligned with positioning |
| **Data overload** | Too many metrics | Focus on 3-5 key metrics weekly |
| **Not acting on insights** | Analysis paralysis | Create 3 action items from each report |
