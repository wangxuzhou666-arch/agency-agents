# Stable Design (稿定设计)

## Overview

Stable Design (稿定设计) is a Chinese graphic design platform offering thousands of templates for marketing materials, social media graphics, business documents, and promotional content, specifically optimized for Chinese platforms including Xiaohongshu, with features like photo editing, font management, and team collaboration.

## When to Use

**Use when**:
- Creating promotional posters and banners
- Designing Xiaohongshu marketing materials
- Making business presentations and documents
- Producing holiday and seasonal graphics
- Creating print-ready materials
- Designing Chinese-focused marketing content
- Need quick, professional templates

**Do NOT use when**:
- Need completely custom, original designs
- Require advanced vector graphics (use Illustrator)
- Want complex photo manipulation (use Photoshop)
- Creating for Western platforms (different aesthetic)
